Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book my photography session?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings and 2-4 weeks for other sessions to ensure availability. Popular dates fill up quickly, so early booking is advised.
What is included in your photography packages?
Our packages include professional photography, edited high-resolution images, online gallery, printing rights, and a personalized consultation. Additional services like albums, prints, and extended coverage are available as add-ons.
Do you travel for photography sessions?
Yes, we travel for sessions! Local travel is included in our packages, and we're available for destination photography with travel expenses calculated based on location.
How long until I receive my photos?
Delivery times vary by session type: Engagement sessions (2-3 weeks), Weddings (4-6 weeks), Portrait sessions (2 weeks). We provide a few preview images within a week of your session.
