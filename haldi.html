Celebrating the Haldi Ceremony

The Haldi ceremony is a beautiful pre-wedding ritual where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom by family members and friends. This auspicious ceremony not only blesses the couple but also creates joyful, colorful moments filled with laughter and love. The vibrant yellow of turmeric symbolizes purity, prosperity, and the beginning of a new life together.
